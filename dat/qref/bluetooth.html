Bluetooth Sniffing

Bluetooth Low Energy and Bluetooth Classic are two independent things. Most newer devices use Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). This guide is BLE sniffing for these devices.

Requirements

Hardware:

  • nRF52840 dongle or something similar (e.g. nRF51822 dongle)

Software:

  • Python
  • Wireshark
  • nRF Sniffer
