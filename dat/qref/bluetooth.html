Bluetooth Sniffing

Bluetooth Low Energy and Bluetooth Classic are two independent things. This guide is about Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) sniffing.

Requirements

You need a card/dongle capable of sniffing. Apparently this company called Nordic Semiconductor is the main player. This nRF52840 dongle seems to be the go-to device for sniffing. I have a nRF51822 dongle that works as well. You need Wireshark, Python, and nRF Sniffer.
